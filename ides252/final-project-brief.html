North Shore Search and Rescue - winter safety

Launch the digital portion of a safety campaign for ski, snowboard, and hiking safety for the local North Shore mountains. North Shore Search and Rescue want to educate outdoor enthusiasts this winter about the risks of hiking, skiing, and snowboarding in the mountains. They want people to be prepared. This blog post explains their recent frustrations. That blog post also includes a ton of helpful tips to stay safe in the mountains this winter. Create a campaign to grab people’s attention and spread the message.

Reboot Movember

The Movember Foundation is looking to reboot their campaign for 2016. Since their inception, they’ve added different ways to get involved. They’ve also added some new areas of support for Men’s Health. This website is where you can learn more. They have found that interest has been waning over the last few years. They need some new ideas to raise awareness and funds for men’s health.
